## Common Terms You Might Encounter

These are terms you may see in articles or conversations. You'll understand them better as you build more.

**State**: When your app remembers things between sessions (like preferences or data).

**API**: How different services talk to each other. Your app might use an API to get weather data or save to a database.

**System Prompt**: Instructions you write that tell Claude how to behave. It shapes the AI's personality and responses.

**CRUD**: Create, Read, Update, Delete — the four basic operations most apps do with data.

**Prompt Engineering**: Shaping how an AI responds by writing better instructions.

**Agent**: An AI that takes actions based on conditions, not just answers questions.

**Frontend**: What users see and interact with (buttons, forms, pages).

**Backend**: Where data is stored and processed (databases, servers).

**Database**: Where your app stores information so it persists.
